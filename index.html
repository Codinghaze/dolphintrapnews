‘SOTC’ to Strike Monday Night

(So-Tock) Officially Declared “Storm of the Century” by… Zach

OSHKOSH, WI — Batten down the hatches, secure your lawn flamingos, and kiss your outdoor furniture goodbye — the Storm of the Century (SOTC) is scheduled to arrive Monday night with enough force to mess up your Netflix queue and your neighbor's trampoline.

Local dad and amateur meteorologist Zachary Dewitt-Moran, who first coined the term SOTC while laughing too hard at his own jokes, was quoted saying,

“It’s definitely gonna be the Storm of the Century… unless there’s a worse one later, in which case this will be retroactively downgraded to 'Storm of the Fiscal Quarter.'”

The National Weather Service, while unable to verify Zach’s authority on storm naming, did acknowledge the incoming system as “pretty slick.”

Residents are advised to stock up on flashlights, canned goods, and a suspicious amount of Diet Coke.
